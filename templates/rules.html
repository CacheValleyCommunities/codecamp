Event Format

Schedule & venue

Duration: 24-hour hackathon format

Schedule:

  • 6:00 PM: Check-in and Opening Ceremony
  • 7:00 PM: Hacking begins
  • 7:00 PM (Next Day): Project submissions close
  • 7:30 PM: Judging and presentations
  • 9:00 PM: Awards ceremony

Venue: Bridgerland Technical College

Categories

Competition categories

App

Mobile or web applications

Game

Video games and interactive experiences

Maker

Hardware projects and IoT solutions

AI

Artificial intelligence and machine learning

Scratch

Visual programming projects for beginners

Participation

Participation rules

  • All participants must be present for the duration of the event
  • Teams can consist of 1-4 members
  • All code must be written during the 24-hour period
  • Open source libraries and frameworks are allowed
  • Projects must be submitted by the deadline
  • Final presentations are mandatory for judging

Participants under 18

Anyone under 18 must have a signed waiver on file and a parent or legal guardian on site for the entire event. Minors receive a yellow wristband at check-in.

Conduct

Code of conduct

We are committed to providing a harassment-free experience for everyone. We do not tolerate harassment in any form.

Expected behavior

  • Be respectful and inclusive
  • Exercise consideration and respect in your speech and actions
  • Attempt collaboration before conflict

Unacceptable behavior: Harassment, discriminatory language, inappropriate content, or any behavior that makes others feel unwelcome will result in immediate disqualification.

Judging

Judging criteria

Innovation

Creativity and originality of the solution

Technical Implementation

Code quality and technical complexity

Problem Solving

How well the project addresses the challenge
