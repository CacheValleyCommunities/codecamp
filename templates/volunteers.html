We are looking for mentors to help participants during the event. If you have experience in coding, design, or any relevant field, we'd love to have you join us.

We are also looking for judges to evaluate projects at the end of the event. If you have expertise in technology or related areas, please consider volunteering.

Opportunities

Volunteer roles

Event Setup

Help prepare the venue, set up signage, arrange seating, and ensure everything is ready.

Registration Support

Welcome attendees, manage check-in, distribute badges and materials, and answer questions.

Technical Assistance

Support A/V equipment, troubleshoot technical issues, and help speakers with presentations.

Workshop Facilitation

Assist workshop leaders and help participants with hands-on activities.

Social Media

Document the event through photos and posts and engage with attendees online.

Event Cleanup

Help break down displays, clean up spaces, and leave the venue in excellent condition.
