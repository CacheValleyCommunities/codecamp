Partner With Us

Partner with us: invest in Northern Utah's tech future at CodeCamp

We are excited to invite you to partner with us for the 2nd Annual Cache Valley CodeCamp, taking place July 10-11 at Bridgerland Technical College. Following a successful inaugural year, this summer's hackathon is designed to foster computer science, creativity, and deep community connection across Northern Utah.

By uniting K-12 students, college tech talent, industry professionals, and hobbyists, our goal is simple: strengthen the local economy, highlight regional educational pathways, and build a robust tech talent pipeline.

A Proven Model

A proven model for regional growth

We know this model works. The Southern Utah Code Camp was launched more than 15 years ago in St. George, where it has since sparked startups, driven massive community growth, and created a premier regional talent pipeline. Board member Brent Dalling joined their committee in 2020, and we are now using that proven blueprint to create similar momentum here in Cache Valley.

Sponsorship opportunities

To keep this event accessible and free for participants, we welcome contributions toward:

  • Volunteer Power (Highest Impact): Provide judges, mentors, and event staff from your team to scout top regional talent directly.
  • Meal Sponsorship: Sponsor breakfast, lunch, or dinner on Day 1 or Day 2.
  • Event Apparel: Sponsor 100 official event shirts with your logo on the back.
  • Awards & Prizes: Support trophies, medals, and giveaway items.
  • Monetary Support: General funding for event operations.

What's in it for you?

In return for your investment, we deliver meaningful brand visibility and engagement:

  • Direct Talent Pipeline: Meet bright students and local professionals.
  • High-Visibility Branding: Website, social, event materials, and on-site digital display placement.
  • Verbal Recognition: Opening and closing ceremony shout-outs.
  • Accountability: Post-event impact summary showing exactly how contributions helped.
