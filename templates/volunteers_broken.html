Volunteer Opportunities

📝
Event Setup

Help prepare the venue, set up signage, arrange seating, and ensure everything is ready for attendees.

🎯
Registration Support

Welcome attendees, manage check-in processes, distribute badges and materials, and answer questions.

🔧
Technical Assistance

Support A/V equipment, troubleshoot technical issues, and help speakers with presentation setup.

👥
Workshop Facilitation

Assist workshop leaders, help participants with hands-on activities, and manage room logistics.

📱
Social Media

Document the event through photos and posts, engage with attendees online, and promote activities.

🧹
Event Cleanup

Help break down displays, clean up spaces, and ensure the venue is left in excellent condition.
