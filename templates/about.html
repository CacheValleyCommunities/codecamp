Our Story

Building Cache Valley's tech community

CodeCamp Bridgerland was founded in 2025 by the Cache Tech Community with the support of Bridgerland Technical College. Our mission is to empower developers, designers, and tech enthusiasts through education, networking, and community building in the form of a yearly hackathon.

The hackathon is open-ended and based on the Southern Utah Code Camp model. In our first year, we had 23 participants, a guest speaker, and 5 sponsors. We are looking forward to growing the event in the coming years.

This event is for anyone interested in technology, coding, and innovation. Whether you're a student, a professional, or just curious about the tech world, CodeCamp Bridgerland welcomes you.

Competition Divisions

Find your division

DayCamp

For: K-12 students. A one-day track designed for kids in a supportive, age-appropriate environment.

Rookie

For: High school students or those without prior experience. Perfect for beginners who want to learn by doing.

Novice

For: College students or those with some experience. Ready for more challenging projects.

Industry

For: Professional developers and experts. The most competitive division.
