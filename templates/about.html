About CodeCamp

Our story, mission, and the people who make it happen

Our Story

CodeCamp Bridgerland was founded in 2025 by the Cache Tech Community with the support of Bridgerland Technical College. Our mission is to empower developers, designers, and tech enthusiasts through education, networking, and community building in the form of a yearly hackathon.

The hackathon is open-ended and based on the Southern Utah Code Camp model. In our first year, which just ended, we had 23 participants, a guest speaker, and 5 sponsors. We are looking forward to growing the event in the coming years.

This event is for anyone interested in technology, coding, and innovation. Whether you're a student, a professional, or just curious about the tech world, CodeCamp Bridgerland welcomes you. Our event is designed to be inclusive and accessible, with categories for all skill levels and ages.

Our Mission

Educate: Provide accessible, high-quality learning opportunities for developers at every stage of their journey.

Connect: Foster meaningful relationships between local professionals, students, and technology enthusiasts.

Inspire: Showcase innovative projects and emerging technologies to spark creativity and growth.

Support: Create a supportive environment where everyone feels welcome to contribute and learn.
